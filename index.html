Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fly? -
Fly is a comprehensive project management platform designed to enhance team productivity and streamline collaboration workflows with its intuitive interface and powerful features.

Fly is available on multiple platforms, including web, iOS, and Android, ensuring your team can manage projects and collaborate from anywhere. Fly is ideal for teams, departments, and organizations looking to improve their project management efficiency.
Is this available to my country? +
Fly is designed to be accessible globally with servers worldwide for optimal performance. You can check our website for specific regional features or contact our support team for availability in your area.
How do I use the features of Fly app? +
To get started with Fly, simply create an account through our website or mobile app. Once registered, you can create your first project, invite team members, set up tasks, and begin collaborating. Our intuitive interface guides you through all the essential project management features.
How much do the Fly app cost? +
Fly offers flexible pricing plans to suit different team sizes and needs. We have a Basic plan at $19 per month for small teams, a Pro plan at $39 per month for growing teams, and a Business plan at $99 per month for large organizations. Each plan includes different features and team member limits.
I have technical problem, what do I email? +
Please email our support team at support@flyapp.com with details about your technical issue. Our dedicated support team will help resolve any problems you encounter with the platform.
